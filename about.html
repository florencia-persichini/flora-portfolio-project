About me.

Get to know me better

I’m a designer and front-end developer with a passion for creating seamless and visually compelling digital experiences. Originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina, I studied Graphic Design, UX/UI Design, and am currently expanding my skills in Front-End Development. Since 2018, I’ve been based in Barcelona, where I continue to grow and evolve in the tech and design world. With experience in both design and customer care, I bring a user-centered approach to my work, ensuring intuitive and engaging digital experiences. I’m a perfectionist with a strong eye for aesthetics, always striving for pixel-perfect designs that balance beauty and functionality. I also have strong soft skills, thrive in team environments, and love collaborating to bring ideas to life.
